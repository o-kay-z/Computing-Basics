Fundamentals Hardware Software Computer Types

Software

Controls the information and operation in the computer. Consists of programs (Application Software/System Software) that allow a computer to carry out specific tasks, distinguishing it from the physical parts of the system, known as hardware Includes a set of instructions, procedures, algorithms, and related documentation that together ensure the smooth operation of a data processing system Software makes it possible for users to accomplish a wide range of tasks, from basic calculations to complex data analysis

Application Software

Used to solve a particular problem or to perform a particular task Applied by users to do real-world task E.g. keep track of inventory, design a car engine, draft minutes for meeting, play games. May either be custom-designed or packaged

System Software

manages and supports the operation of a computer system, often interacting directly with hardware. Includes operating systems, hardware drivers, and programming languages, which are typically not of concern to regular end-users.

Implementation in Communities

Education

Computer-Based Education (CBE) is implemented in order for allow institutions and teachers to control, deliver, and evaluate the studentâ€™s learning. It helps institutions track and analyze student performance.

Healthcare

Used for patient record-keeping, diagnostics, and medical procedures, including monitoring systems, lab diagnostics, and surgeries. (Electronic Health Record Software)

Communication

Computers are used for Email, chatting, Usenet, FTP, Telnet, and video conferencing.

Insurance

Insurance companies use computers to maintain up-to-date records of clients, such as start dates, due installments, maturity dates, interest, survival benefits, and bonuses. (AMS/PMS)

Engineering

Computer-Aided Design (CAD) used in various engineering fields, including structural engineering for stress and strain analysis, industrial engineering for system design and improvement, and architectural engineering for town planning and building design using 2D and 3D drawings.

Government

Computers are used for budgeting, sales and income tax departments, gender ratio calculations, voter list management, PAN card issuance, and weather forecasting. (PMS)

Military

Computers are heavily used in defense for controlling modern tanks, missiles, weapons, and military systems. Examples: missile control, military communication, operation and planning, and smart weapons communication.
